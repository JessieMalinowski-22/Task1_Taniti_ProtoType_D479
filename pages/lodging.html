Where to Stay in Taniti

Whether you're looking for luxury, family-friendly comfort, or a budget-friendly stay, Taniti Island offers a variety of lodging options to suit every traveler's needs.

Luxury Resorts

Experience the finest amenities with ocean views, spas, on-site dining, and private beach access.

Family Hotels

Comfortable, kid-friendly accommodations with pools, family suites, and easy access to local attractions.

Bed & Breakfasts

Quaint and cozy B&Bs hosted by local residents offer personalized hospitality and homemade breakfasts.

Budget Hostels

Affordable options for solo travelers and backpackers, many within walking distance of the beach and bus stops.
